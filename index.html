Frequently Asked Questions

How do I mark attendance?

Go to the Attendance section and use the camera to capture your photo. The system will automatically mark your attendance with location verification.

How to apply for leave?

Navigate to the Leave Management section, click "Apply Leave", fill in the details and submit. Your HR will review and approve.

What if I forget to mark attendance?

Contact your HR/admin to mark attendance manually. You'll need to provide a valid reason for the missed attendance.
