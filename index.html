FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know before we start.

How far in advance should we book? +
We recommend booking 6–12 months ahead, especially for wedding dates during peak season (November–February). However, we do accommodate last‑minute inquiries based on availability.
Do you travel for destination shoots? +
Absolutely! We’ve shot across India, Europe, and Southeast Asia. Travel and accommodation costs are added to the package, and we handle all logistics.
How many photos will we receive? +
Depending on the package, you’ll receive between 200 to 400+ high‑resolution edited images. We believe in quality over quantity — every delivered photo is meticulously edited and color graded.
What is your editing style? +
Our signature style is timeless, warm, and cinematic with true‑to‑life colors. We avoid heavy filters that date quickly, ensuring your memories look fresh forever.
Can we get raw files? +
Raw files are not included in standard packages as they are unfinished work. But we can discuss providing them under a special agreement.
Do you offer Haldi, Mehndi, and Sangeet coverage? +
Yes, our Premium Luxury package fully covers Haldi, Mehndi, Sangeet, and the Main Wedding event. For other packages, we can customize based on your requirements.
What happens if the weather is bad? +
We always have backup indoor locations and lighting arrangements. We shoot in all weather conditions to ensure you get the perfect shots.
How do we pay the advance? +
We accept bank transfers, UPI, or cash. A 30% advance is required to confirm your booking, and the remaining amount is paid as per the schedule mentioned in the terms.
Do you provide drone footage? +
Absolutely. Drone coverage is standard in our Gold and Premium Luxury packages, but can also be added as an additional service to the Silver package for ₹5,000.
How can I book my date? +
You can directly DM us on Instagram, call us at 7654200109, or fill out the contact form below. We will immediately check availability and guide you through the booking process.
