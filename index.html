Our Philosophy

At The Wedding, we believe every frame is a canvas for emotions. Founded in 2004, we’ve grown from a small passion project into a full‑service creative powerhouse, delivering world‑class visual stories across weddings, commercial campaigns, and editorial shoots.

Our team combines technical mastery with artistic intuition, ensuring that each project isn’t just a collection of images, but a timeless narrative. We obsess over light, composition, and the subtle moments that others miss.

Based in Mumbai but working globally, we’ve had the privilege of documenting over 1200 stories — and we’re just getting started.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before we start.

How far in advance should we book? +
We recommend booking 6–12 months ahead, especially for wedding dates during peak season (November–February). However, we do accommodate last‑minute inquiries based on availability.
Do you travel for destination shoots? +
Absolutely! We’ve shot across India, Europe, and Southeast Asia. Travel and accommodation costs are added to the package, and we handle all logistics.
How many photos will we receive? +
Depending on the package, you’ll receive between 100 to unlimited high‑resolution edited images. We believe in quality over quantity — every delivered photo is meticulously edited.
What is your editing style? +
Our signature style is timeless, warm, and cinematic with true‑to‑life colors. We avoid heavy filters that date quickly, ensuring your memories look fresh forever.
Can we get raw files? +
Raw files are not included in standard packages as they are unfinished work. But we can discuss providing them under a special agreement.
