FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know before we start your journey together.

How far in advance should we book? +
We recommend booking 6–12 months ahead, especially for wedding dates during peak season (November–February). However, we do accommodate last‑minute inquiries based on availability.
Do you travel for destination shoots? +
Absolutely! We’ve shot across India, Europe, and Southeast Asia. Travel and accommodation costs are added to the package, and we handle all logistics.
How many photos will we receive? +
Depending on the package, you’ll receive between 200 to 400+ high‑resolution edited images. We believe in quality over quantity — every delivered photo is meticulously edited and color graded.
What is your editing style? +
Our signature style is timeless, warm, and cinematic with true‑to‑life colors. We avoid heavy filters that date quickly, ensuring your memories look fresh forever.
Can we get raw files? +
Raw files are not included in standard packages as they are unfinished work. But we can discuss providing them under a special agreement.
Do you offer Haldi, Mehndi, and Sangeet coverage? +
Yes, our Premium Luxury package fully covers Haldi, Mehndi, Sangeet, and the Main Wedding event. For other packages, we can customize based on your requirements.
What happens if the weather is bad? +
We always have backup indoor locations and lighting arrangements. We shoot in all weather conditions to ensure you get the perfect shots.
How do we pay the advance? +
We accept bank transfers, UPI, or cash. A 30% advance is required to confirm your booking, and the remaining amount is paid as per the schedule mentioned in the terms.
Do you provide drone footage? +
Absolutely. Drone coverage is standard in our Gold and Premium Luxury packages, but can also be added as an additional service to the Silver package for ₹5,000.
How can I book my date? +
You can click the "Book Now" button anywhere on our website, select your preferred date from the popup calendar, and it will automatically send a WhatsApp message to us with your chosen date!
Do you offer pre-wedding shoots? +
Yes, pre-wedding shoots are part of our Gold and Luxury packages. We also offer standalone pre-wedding packages for couples who want to capture their story before the big day.
How many photographers will be on my wedding day? +
We provide a team of 2 to 4 photographers and cinematographers depending on your chosen package, ensuring every angle is perfectly covered.
What is your turnaround time for delivery? +
Our standard delivery time is 15 to 30 days from the date of your wedding. For special requests like Same Day Edit, we can do that at an additional cost.
Can we customize a package? +
Yes! We love creating custom packages. Just tell us your requirements, budget, and dates, and we will tailor a special plan just for you.
Do you have a studio in Patna? +
Yes, we are based in Patna City, Bihar. You can visit our office at Mahadev Sthan, Marchi Road, near Bypass Thana, Patna City - 800009.
How do I get a quote quickly? +
Simply click the "Book Now" button on our website. It will open a date picker. Select your date and we'll give you a quote immediately on WhatsApp!
What is included in the album design? +
Our albums are premium leather-bound, 12x36 inches, with 40-50 sheets. The design is custom-made based on your wedding theme. Additional albums are available at extra cost.
Do you offer cinematic editing for short reels? +
Yes! We create Instagram-friendly reels, teasers (1 minute), and highlight videos (5-10 minutes) that are perfect for social media sharing.
Are you available for out-of-station shoots? +
Yes, we are available for out-of-station and international shoots. Travel and accommodation expenses will be handled as per actuals.
Do you charge extra for overtime? +
Our packages cover the full day of the event. However, if the timeline extends significantly beyond the standard schedule, an additional hourly charge may apply.
